Oh My Trip!

Oh my Trip is a travel app that I created to help the post pandemic travelers plan their trip.

We developed a travel app that helps modern travelers plan their post pandemic trips by providing them with assistance from start to finish by giving them information needed e.g PCR tests, different airports requirements, accommodation options in order for them to have the best experience.

Background

User Research

User Interview

We first took a qualitative approach through one-on-one interviews in order to help us understand the thought process, emotions, and opinions of our users when planning a trip in a post pandemic era. We also used a quantitative approach by creating a survey through Google form. We sent the survey to 35 participants; both male and female ranging from 24 to 55 year olds. This quantitative approach will help us get actionable data and help us create our user persona.

Affinity Diagram

After the one*one interviews with the users and the survey data, we added all the user insights into different colorful sticky notes. Then, we split those notes into group similarities, defines the topics for each category, and then we got our final affinity diagram. This will help us fulfill our customer needs while targeting specific aspects that they mentioned during the interview. The survey will help into undterstanding better our target.

User Persona

After categorizing all user's insights and put them into an affinity diagram, we created an empathy map that includes some of the quotes the user said during the interview, what the user thinks, does and also feels. We also highlighted some pain points and grain that most users mentioned. After creating the empathy map, we then created a user persona and added the most important information to keep in mind while developing the product such as interests, bio, goals and needs.

Definition & Ideation

Problem Statement

- Pain Point? Finding low budget trips with interesting activities while being safe.
- Impact Statement? To live the best travel experience without being stressed.

Our travel app was designed to help modern travelers plan a safe post pandemic trip at a low budget. We have discovered that the majority of users we interviewed had a hard time planning a low budget trip with interesting activities and were so stressed about COVID-19 regulations. How might we help modern travelers live their best post pandemic travel experience without being stressed about safety concerns?

Value Proposition

We are better because we assist you from start to finish.
We are believable because we care about our users satisfaction.

Our new travel startup is developing a mobile app to help modern travelers find the best low budget post pandemic trip while ensuring safety and fun.
